New Bharath Printing Press , is a printing and packaging services company in Vandiperiyar established in 1970's to serve the requirements for business stationery, marketing material, promotional paraphernalia, event articles, gift packing, wedding invitation cards, and personalized items.

The hallmark of our success is our belief in three values: Service, Quality and Price. We believe in meeting customer specifications and earning their delight while keeping our price competitive for the quality of work, material and expertise we render.

We work with a wide spectrum of customers ranging from large corporate houses, small and medium businesses, product designers, online marketeers and individuals.

We are a one-stop shop for all printing requirements under one umbrella. From prepress, printing, final finishing to binding, all done under one roof in our premises in Vandiperiyar.

We excel in meeting challenges, both in terms of craftsmanship and time-frames. Customers trust us for our ability to deliver on commitments.

We can say with pride that a new bharath customer is loyal and comes back again and also recommends us to friends and colleagues.

We call ourselves the Most Preferred Printer in town.
